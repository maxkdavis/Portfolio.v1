Hi! I'm Max, a frontend web developer

I approach web development as a blend of creativity and critical thinking. I strive to create efficient and elegant code while being mindful of aesthetics, functionality, and user experiences.

My childhood was spent with countless hours of gaming, completing puzzles, drawing mazes and building legos. This joy of problem-solving is a big part of who I am and it's what attracted me to web development. It's fulfilling to be able to express my creativity while building an experience that can benefit others.

One of the most exciting aspects of web development is the continuous pursuit of learning. As user preferences, conventions, mediums, and tools continue to evolve, we must also adapt to modern technologies in order to address new challenges. I'm thrilled to be a part of the journey.
